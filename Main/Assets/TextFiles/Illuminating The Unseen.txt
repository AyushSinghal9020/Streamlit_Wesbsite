# Illuminating the Unseen: Emphasizing the ability to reveal something previously unknown.

As many of us have experienced, `visiting healthcare professionals` often involves encountering `complex terminology`, `medical concepts`, and `numerical data`. Unfortunately, studies indicate that **`40-80% of this vital information is forgotten immediately`**, potentially `hindering understanding` and `adherence to treatment plans`.

This $Project$ seeks to `bridge the gap` between `patients and their medical information` by addressing `this critical issue`.
